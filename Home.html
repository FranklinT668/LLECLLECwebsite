Objectives:

Language, Literature and Education Centre (LLEC) is founded on the basis of Dr. Judy's Writing Classroom and aims to promote lifelong learning by exploring the values of language, literature and education. It provides consulting and editing services, tutoring and classes and English learning & speaking clubs for both young and adult learners.
